# Play Store Listing - Captr

## App Name
Captr - Business Card Scanner

## Short Description (80 chars max)
Scan business cards instantly with AI. Extract, organize & export contacts.

## Full Description (4000 chars max)
Captr is the smartest way to digitize your business cards. Simply snap a photo of any business card and our AI-powered OCR technology instantly extracts all contact details - names, phone numbers, emails, websites, addresses, and more.

KEY FEATURES:

- AI-Powered Scanning: Take a photo or import from gallery. Our GPT-4o powered OCR accurately reads cards in any language and layout.

- Front & Back Support: Capture both sides of a card for complete information extraction.

- Smart Contact Management: All your scanned contacts are organized and searchable in one place.

- AI Chat Assistant: Ask questions about your contacts, find people by company or role, and get networking insights powered by AI.

- Cloud Sync: Your contacts are securely stored in the cloud and accessible from anywhere.

- Export Options: Export your contacts as Excel or CSV files for easy sharing and backup.

- Review & Edit: Review extracted data before saving and make corrections if needed.

SECURITY & PRIVACY:
- All data encrypted in transit
- User-scoped data - your contacts are private to your account
- Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in
- No data sold to third parties

Whether you are at a conference, networking event, or business meeting, Captr helps you capture and organize every connection effortlessly.
