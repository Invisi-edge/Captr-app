Privacy Policy

Captr - Business Card Scanner · Last updated: February 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Captr, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you create an account or sign in with Google.
  • Business Card Data: Photos of business cards you scan, and extracted contact information (name, phone, email, company, job title, website, address).
  • Device Information: Camera access (only when actively scanning) and photo library access (only when importing images).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • OCR Processing: Card images are sent to OpenAI API for text extraction. Images are processed in real-time and are not stored by OpenAI beyond the API request.
  • Data Storage: Extracted contact information and card images are stored in Google Cloud (Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage) associated with your user account.
  • AI Chat: Your saved contacts are used to power the in-app AI assistant, which helps you search and manage your contacts.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Cloud Platform infrastructure (Firebase). All data is:

  • Encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Scoped to your authenticated account - other users cannot access your data
  • Stored in data centers that comply with industry security standards

4. Third-Party Services

Captr uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and file storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: Business card text extraction (OCR). OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication method. Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide app functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your stored data through the app
  • Edit or delete any saved contact cards
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • Export your contacts at any time

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data (contacts, card images) will be permanently removed within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Captr is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
