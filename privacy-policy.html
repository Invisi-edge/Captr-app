Privacy Policy

Captr - Business Card Scanner · Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Captr, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Business card images: Photos you take or select from your gallery for scanning.
  • Extracted contact data: Names, phone numbers, email addresses, job titles, company names, websites, and addresses extracted from scanned cards.
  • Chat messages: Messages you send to our AI assistant (not stored permanently).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To scan business cards and extract contact details using AI-powered OCR.
  • To store and organize your scanned contacts for easy retrieval.
  • To provide AI chat assistance for searching and managing your contacts.
  • To enable data export (Excel/CSV) of your contacts.
  • To authenticate your account and secure your data.

3. Third-Party Services

Captr uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For user authentication, database storage (Firestore), and file storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI: For business card OCR (text extraction from images) and AI chat functionality. Card images are sent to OpenAI for processing but are not used to train their models. OpenAI Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure. We use Firebase Authentication to protect your account and ensure only you can access your data. All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS.

5. Data Retention

Your contact data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete individual contacts at any time from within the app. To delete your entire account and all associated data, please contact us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, export, and download your scanned contact data.
  • Delete individual contacts or request full account deletion.
  • Withdraw consent and stop using the service at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

Captr is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy within the app.
