Privacy Policy

Captr - Business Card Scanner · Last updated: February 2026 · India

Captr ("we", "our", or "us") is an India-based application. This Privacy Policy is drafted in compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000, the IT (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures) Rules, 2011, and the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 of India.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Captr, we collect the following personal data with your consent:

  • Account information: Email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Business card images: Photos you take or select from your gallery for scanning.
  • Extracted contact data: Names, phone numbers, email addresses, job titles/designations, company names, websites, addresses, and other details (such as GST numbers) extracted from scanned cards.
  • Chat messages: Messages you send to our AI assistant (processed in real-time, not stored permanently).
  • Device information: Device type and operating system version for app compatibility purposes only.

2. Purpose and Lawful Basis for Processing DPDP Act

We process your personal data only for the following specific purposes, based on your explicit consent:

  • To scan business cards and extract contact details using AI-powered OCR.
  • To store and organise your scanned contacts for easy retrieval.
  • To provide AI chat assistance for searching and managing your contacts.
  • To enable data export (Excel/CSV) of your contacts.
  • To authenticate your account and secure your data.

We do not process your data for profiling, targeted advertising, or any purpose beyond what is described above.

3. Third-Party Data Processors

Captr uses the following third-party services as data processors:

  • Firebase (Google LLC): For user authentication, database storage (Firestore), and file storage. Data may be stored on Google servers. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI (OpenAI, Inc.): For business card OCR (text extraction from images) and AI chat functionality. Card images are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. OpenAI does not use API data to train their models. OpenAI Privacy Policy

Cross-border data transfer: Some of your data may be processed by servers located outside India (Firebase and OpenAI infrastructure). By using Captr, you consent to this transfer in accordance with the DPDP Act, 2023.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your contact data is retained only as long as your account is active. You have the right to:

  • Delete individual contacts at any time from within the app.
  • Request complete account and data deletion by contacting us.

Upon account deletion, all your personal data will be permanently erased from our systems within 30 days.

6. Your Rights Under Indian Law DPDP Act

As a Data Principal under the DPDP Act, 2023, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can access and export all your scanned contact data at any time.
  • Right to Correction: You can edit and correct any extracted contact information.
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete individual contacts or request full account deletion.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent and stop using the service at any time. Withdrawal of consent will not affect the lawfulness of processing done prior to withdrawal.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: You can raise a grievance with our Grievance Officer (details below).
  • Right to Nominate: You have the right to nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity.

7. Children's Privacy

Captr is not directed at children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party. Your data is only shared with the third-party processors listed in Section 3, solely for providing the app's core functionality.
